How to use

Paste-in integration for the three most common setups.

1
iOS · Xcode

Replace your asset catalog’s AppIcon.appiconset/ with the one in the zip. Single-source 1024 — Xcode 14+ generates every size.

cp -R ios/AppIcon.appiconset/ <App>/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/
2
Android · Studio

Copy mipmap-* into app/src/main/res/. Themed icon is wired automatically via the anydpi-v26 manifest.

cp -R android/mipmap-* app/src/main/res/
3
Web · <head>

Drop web/ contents at your site root and paste the <head> snippet from web/usage.html.

cp -R web/* <site-root>/
